Guidelines

Logging Services is a project of the Apache Software Foundation (ASF) and composed of several subprojects; Log4cxx, Log4j, Log4Net, etc. Logging Services Project Management Committee (PMC) is responsible for the management of the project and all its subprojects. Committers and PMC members are elected due to merit for the evolution of the project. Changes and releases are subject to voting.
